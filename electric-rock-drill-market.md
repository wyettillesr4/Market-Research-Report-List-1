<p><p>The electric rock drill market is primarily categorized into two types: pneumatic and hydraulic rock drills. Pneumatic rock drills operate using compressed air, making them lightweight and efficient for various drilling applications, especially in construction and mining. Conversely, hydraulic rock drills utilize hydraulic pressure, providing higher power and torque for more demanding tasks. Both types serve distinct purposes, with pneumatic drills favored for portability and speed, while hydraulic drills are preferred for heavy-duty operations requiring greater force and stability.</p></p>
