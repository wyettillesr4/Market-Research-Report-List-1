<p><p>The ski goggles market includes three main types of lenses: ordinary lenses, myopic lenses, and presbyopic glasses. Ordinary lenses are designed for general vision correction and UV protection, making them suitable for most skiers. Myopic lenses cater specifically to individuals with nearsightedness, providing enhanced clarity on the slopes. Presbyopic glasses, on the other hand, address age-related vision changes, allowing skiers to see both near and far clearly. Each type serves distinct user needs, enhancing the overall skiing experience.</p></p>
