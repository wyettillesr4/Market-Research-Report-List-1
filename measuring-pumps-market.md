<p><ul><li>Fixed Displacement Measuring Pumps</li><li>Variable Displacement Measuring Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The measuring pumps market consists of two primary types: fixed displacement and variable displacement measuring pumps. Fixed displacement measuring pumps deliver a consistent volume of fluid with each stroke, ensuring precise dosing for applications requiring accuracy. In contrast, variable displacement measuring pumps can adjust the fluid volume per stroke, offering flexibility for varying operational needs. Both types are essential in industries like pharmaceuticals, chemicals, and food processing, where accurate measurement and control of fluid transfer are critical for maintaining quality and efficiency.</p></p>
