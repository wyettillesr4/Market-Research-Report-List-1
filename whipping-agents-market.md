Ionic whipping agents, in contrast, rely on charged particles to stabilize foams, enhancing the performance of whipped products. Each type plays a crucial role in various applications, including baked goods, dairy, and confectionery, by influencing viscosity, foaming capacity, and overall product stability.</p></p>
