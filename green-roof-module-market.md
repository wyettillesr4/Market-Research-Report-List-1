<p><p>The green roof module market consists of three primary types: Extensive, Semi-Intensive, and Intensive green roof modules. Extensive modules are lightweight and feature low-maintenance vegetation, ideal for flat rooftops. Semi-Intensive modules offer a mix of low and medium-height plantings, requiring moderate maintenance and providing additional aesthetic value. Intensive modules are the heaviest, supporting a wide variety of plants, including shrubs and small trees, necessitating regular upkeep and irrigation, making them suitable for gardens and recreation spaces on rooftops.</p></p>
