<p><strong>The Bionic Exoskeletons Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Exoskeletons</li><li>Passive Exoskeletons</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bionic exoskeleton market is divided into active and passive exoskeletons. Active exoskeletons utilize motors and sensors to assist and enhance the user's strength and mobility dynamically, often used in rehabilitation and industrial settings. Conversely, passive exoskeletons rely on springs and rigid structures to redistribute body weight and support movements without external power, primarily designed to reduce fatigue in workers and enhance endurance. Both types aim to improve human capabilities and reduce physical strain in various applications.</p></p>
