<p><strong>The Automatic Blasting Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hanger Type</li><li>Tumblast Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic blasting machine market features two primary types: hanger type and tumblast type. Hanger type machines utilize overhead systems to suspend components, allowing for uniform abrasive blasting from multiple angles simultaneously, ideal for larger parts. In contrast, tumblast type machines use a tumbling action to expose workpieces to abrasive materials, ensuring thorough cleaning and finishing for smaller, irregularly shaped items. Both types enhance efficiency and productivity in surface preparation across various industries, including automotive and aerospace.</p></p>
