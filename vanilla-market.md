<p><ul><li>Bean</li><li>Extract</li><li>Powder</li><li>Paste</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vanilla market encompasses various forms including beans, extracts, powders, and pastes. Vanilla beans are the whole pods containing natural flavor compounds, prized for their rich aroma. Extracts are produced by soaking the beans in alcohol, capturing their essence for use in beverages and baking. Powders are finely ground beans or extracts, often utilized in cooking and baking for convenience. Lastly, paste combines the essence of beans and a binder, providing a concentrated flavor, ideal for desserts and pastries.</p></p>
