<p><p>The handheld explosives detectors market is primarily categorized into two types: trace detectors and steam detectors. Trace detectors identify minute particles of explosives, often through swabs or air sampling, making them effective for security checks at various locations. Steam detectors, on the other hand, utilize vapor analysis to sense the presence of explosive materials. Both types serve crucial roles in enhancing safety and security in areas like airports, military installations, and public events, ensuring swift detection and response to threats.</p></p>
