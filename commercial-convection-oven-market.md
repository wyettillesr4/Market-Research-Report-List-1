<p><p>The commercial convection oven market is primarily categorized into two types: countertop convection ovens and floor model convection ovens. Countertop convection ovens are compact and suitable for smaller kitchen spaces, offering versatility for baking, roasting, and heating. They are ideal for restaurants and cafes with limited space. In contrast, floor model convection ovens are larger, designed for high-volume operations, and can accommodate more food at once. These ovens are typically used in larger food service establishments, providing efficient cooking and consistent results.</p></p>
