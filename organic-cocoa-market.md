<p><ul><li>Cocoa Liquor</li><li>Cocoa Powder</li><li>Cocoa Butter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The organic cocoa market encompasses three primary segments: cocoa liquor, cocoa powder, and cocoa butter. Cocoa liquor, a thick paste from ground cocoa beans, serves as a foundation for chocolate products. Cocoa powder, derived from pressing cocoa liquor, is used in baking and beverages, providing rich flavor. Cocoa butter, the fat extracted from cocoa beans, enhances texture and creaminess in chocolates and cosmetics. Together, these products cater to increasing consumer demand for organic and sustainable cocoa options in various industries.</p></p>
