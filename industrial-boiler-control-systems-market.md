<p><ul><li>Modulating Control</li><li>On/Off Control</li><li>High-fire/Low-fire Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial boiler control systems market encompasses three primary types: Modulating Control, On/Off Control, and High-fire/Low-fire Control. Modulating Control adjusts the fuel and air inputs continuously to maintain optimal combustion efficiency, enhancing performance and reducing emissions. On/Off Control operates the boiler at full capacity or shuts it down, making it simple but less efficient. High-fire/Low-fire Control provides two operational levels, allowing for better energy management than On/Off while improving efficiency in varying load conditions.</p></p>
