<p><ul><li>PVA</li><li>BOPP</li><li>LDPE</li><li>PET</li><li>PA</li><li>CPP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plastic membrane market comprises various types, each serving distinct applications. PVA (Polyvinyl Alcohol) membranes are known for their water solubility and biodegradability, making them ideal for packaging and agricultural films. BOPP (Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene) is favored for its clarity and strength, commonly used in flexible packaging. LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) offers flexibility and moisture resistance, suitable for bags and films. PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) boasts excellent tensile strength and durability, often used in food packaging. PA (Polyamide) membranes are recognized for their barrier properties, while CPP (Cast Polypropylene) provides a versatile option for various packaging needs.</p></p>
