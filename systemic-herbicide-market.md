<p><p>The systemic herbicide market is divided into two main types: selective and non-selective herbicides. Selective herbicides target specific weeds while leaving desirable plants unharmed, making them ideal for crops and lawns. In contrast, non-selective herbicides kill all vegetation they come into contact with, which is useful for clearing land and controlling invasive species. Both types play crucial roles in agricultural practices, landscaping, and environmental management, offering tailored solutions for various weed control needs.</p></p>
