<p><ul><li>Active Heatsink</li><li>Passive Heatsink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heatsink market is divided into two main types: active heatsinks and passive heatsinks. Active heatsinks utilize fans or other mechanical components to enhance airflow and improve heat dissipation, making them suitable for high-performance applications like CPUs and GPUs. In contrast, passive heatsinks rely solely on natural convection and conduction, featuring larger surface areas without moving parts, making them ideal for lower-power devices where noise reduction is essential. Both types play crucial roles in thermal management across various industries.</p></p>
