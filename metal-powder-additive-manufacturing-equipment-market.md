<p><p>Metal Powder Additive Manufacturing Equipment encompasses various technologies such as Selective Laser Melting (SLM) and Electron Beam Melting (EBM). SLM utilizes a high-powered laser to fuse metal powder layer by layer, producing intricate components with high density and precision. EBM employs an electron beam in a vacuum environment, enabling the melting of metal powders, ideal for applications requiring a larger build volume and improved thermal properties. Other methods may include Binder Jetting and Direct Metal Laser Sintering (DMLS), each offering unique advantages for specific use cases.</p></p>
