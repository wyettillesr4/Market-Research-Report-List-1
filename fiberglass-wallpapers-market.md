<p><p>Fiberglass wallpapers are categorized into two main types: non-woven and woven fabric. Non-woven fiberglass wallpapers are made from synthetic fibers, providing excellent breathability, durability, and easy application. They are resistant to moisture, making them ideal for humid environments. Woven fiberglass wallpapers, on the other hand, consist of interlaced fibers, offering a more textured and robust finish. These wallpapers provide enhanced aesthetic appeal and superior strength, making them suitable for high-traffic areas while showcasing unique designs and patterns.</p></p>
