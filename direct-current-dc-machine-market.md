DC generators convert mechanical energy into electrical energy, typically used in applications requiring stable voltage output, such as backup power systems. In contrast, DC motors transform electrical energy into mechanical energy, widely utilized in electric vehicles and industrial machinery for precise speed and torque control. Both types are crucial in various industries, driving innovation and efficiency in power conversion and motion control applications.</p></p>
