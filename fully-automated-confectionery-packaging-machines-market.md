<p><p>The fully automated confectionery packaging machines market features two primary types: semi-automatic and fully automatic machines. In contrast, fully automatic machines operate independently, performing all packaging tasks—from product handling to sealing—without human intervention. These machines enhance production speed, reduce labor costs, and ensure higher precision in packaging, catering to the growing demand for efficiency in the confectionery industry.</p></p>
