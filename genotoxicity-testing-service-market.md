<p><p>The Genotoxicity Testing Service Market is segmented into two main types: In Vivo and In Vitro tests. In Vivo tests involve assessing the genotoxic effects of substances within living organisms, providing a comprehensive understanding of how compounds interact at the biological level. In contrast, In Vitro tests are conducted in controlled laboratory environments using cell cultures or tissues to evaluate genetic toxicity without the complexities of a whole organism. Both methods are crucial for risk assessment and regulatory compliance in various industries, including pharmaceuticals and chemicals.</p></p>
