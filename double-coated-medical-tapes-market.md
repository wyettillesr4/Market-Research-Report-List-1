<p><p>The double-coated medical tapes market comprises various material types, each offering unique properties. Polyethylene (PE) tapes are lightweight and flexible, ideal for sensitive skin. Polypropylene (PP) tapes offer excellent adhesion and conformability, enhancing usability in wound care.
