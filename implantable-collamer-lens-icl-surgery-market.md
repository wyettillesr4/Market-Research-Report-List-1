<p><p>Implantable Collamer Lens (ICL) Surgery is an advanced vision correction procedure that involves the implantation of a biocompatible lens inside the eye, specifically between the iris and the natural lens. The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.7% during the forecast period, reflecting a vibrant demand for innovative eye care solutions.</p><p>Key trends shaping this market include the development of multifocal ICLs, enhanced surgical techniques, and growing acceptance of ICL as a safe and reversible option for vision correction. <p><p>Implantable Collamer Lens (ICL) surgery is gaining traction in vision correction markets for myopia, farsightedness, and astigmatism. Myopia surgery addresses nearsightedness by placing the ICL behind the iris, providing clear distance vision. Farsightedness surgery improves the ability to see close objects, utilizing a similar ICL placement. Astigmatism surgery corrects irregular corneal shape by aligning the lens to stabilize vision. The increasing demand for effective and reversible vision correction options drives growth across these specialized markets, catering to diverse patient needs.</p></p>
