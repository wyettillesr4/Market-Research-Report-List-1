<p><p>The single-phase induction motor market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **Capacitor-Start Induction Motor**: Features a capacitor in the start winding, providing high starting torque. </p><p>2. **Capacitor-Run Induction Motor**: Utilizes a capacitor throughout its operation for improved efficiency and performance. </p><p>3. **Resistance Split-Phase Motor**: Employs resistors to create a phase shift for starting, offering moderate starting torque. </p><p>4. **Others**: This category encompasses specialized or less common induction motor types, catering to specific applications or industries.</p></p>
