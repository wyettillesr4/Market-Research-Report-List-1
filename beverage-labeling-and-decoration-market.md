<p><p>The beverage labeling and decoration market includes various types that enhance packaging. Pressure-sensitive labeling utilizes adhesive labels that adhere easily to bottles. Glue-applied labeling involves applying labels with an adhesive that bonds them securely. Sleeve labeling involves heat-shrinkable materials that conform to the container's shape. In-mold labeling integrates labels into the molding process for a seamless look. Other types may include digital printing or embossing methods, offering additional customization and branding opportunities for beverage products.</p></p>
