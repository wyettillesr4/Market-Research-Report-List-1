<p><strong>The Crystal Growing System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Czochralski (CZ) Method Furnace</li><li>Floating Zone (FZ) Method Furnace</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Crystal Growing System market typically includes two main types: the Czochralski (CZ) Method Furnace and the Floating Zone (FZ) Method Furnace. The CZ method involves melting high-purity materials and controlling the crystallization by pulling a seed crystal from the melt, resulting in large single crystals, ideal for semiconductor applications. In contrast, the FZ method uses a localized heat source to melt a small zone of a rod, allowing for precise control and high purity, suitable for advanced electronic components.</p></p>
