<p><p>The Eutectic Al-Si alloy market is categorized into single eutectic and hypereutectic types. Single eutectic alloys contain approximately 12% silicon, offering optimal fluidity and excellent mechanical properties, making them ideal for applications like automotive components. Hypereutectic alloys, with silicon content exceeding 12%, are known for improved wear resistance and lower thermal expansion, making them suitable for high-performance applications. The choice between these types depends on specific application requirements, influencing their performance and cost-efficiency in various industries.</p></p>
