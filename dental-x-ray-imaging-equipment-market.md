<p><strong>The Dental X-Ray Imaging Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intraoral X-ray Imaging</li><li>Extraoral X-ray Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental X-ray imaging equipment market is categorized into intraoral and extraoral imaging types. Intraoral X-ray imaging involves placing the film or sensor inside the patient's mouth, allowing for detailed views of individual teeth and surrounding structures, which aids in diagnosing issues such as cavities and periodontal disease. Extraoral X-ray imaging, on the other hand, captures dental images from outside the mouth, providing broader views useful for assessing jaw structures, oral pathology, and treatment planning. Both types are essential for comprehensive dental care.</p></p>
