<p><p>The novel functional coatings market encompasses various specialized types designed to enhance surfaces. Repellant coatings create barriers against water, dirt, and stains, improving cleanliness. Hydrophilic coatings attract moisture, making them useful in applications like self-cleaning surfaces. Antimicrobial coatings inhibit the growth of bacteria and fungi, promoting hygiene. Protective coatings shield surfaces from abrasion and corrosion, extending their lifespan. Optical coatings enhance the transmission and reflection of light, optimizing performance in devices like lenses and screens, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
