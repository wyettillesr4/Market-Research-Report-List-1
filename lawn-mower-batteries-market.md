<p><p>The lawn mower battery market primarily consists of two main types: lead-acid batteries and lithium-ion (Li-Ion) batteries. Lead-acid batteries are traditional, cost-effective, and widely used, offering reliable performance but heavier weight and shorter lifespan. In contrast, Li-Ion batteries are gaining popularity due to their lightweight, compact design, longer life, and faster charging capabilities, making them ideal for modern, electric-powered lawn mowers. As the demand for efficiency and sustainability grows, the shift towards Li-Ion batteries is becoming more pronounced.</p></p>
