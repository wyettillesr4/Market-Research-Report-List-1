<p><p>Optically Pumped Magnetometers (OPMs) are sensitive devices used to measure magnetic fields through the manipulation of alkali metal vapors, primarily potassium and cesium. The potassium magnetometer utilizes potassium atoms, offering higher sensitivity and lower power consumption, making it suitable for portable applications. In contrast, cesium magnetometers are known for their stability and precision, commonly used in geophysical surveys and medical imaging. Both types contribute significantly to advancements in various fields, including navigation, environmental monitoring, and security technologies.</p></p>
