<p><p>The stainless steel knife gate valves market comprises two primary types: metal seated knife gate valves and resilient seated knife gate valves. Metal seated valves are designed for high-temperature and high-pressure applications, offering durability and resistance to wear. In contrast, resilient seated knives feature a softer sealing material, providing a tight seal even in low-pressure conditions, making them suitable for a variety of fluids. Both types serve critical roles in industries such as water treatment, mining, and pulp and paper.</p></p>
