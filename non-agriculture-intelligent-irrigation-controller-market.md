<p><p>The Non-Agriculture Intelligent Irrigation Controller market is categorized into two main types: weather-based controllers and sensor-based controllers. Weather-based controllers utilize real-time weather data to adjust irrigation schedules, ensuring optimal water use based on conditions like temperature and rainfall. Conversely, sensor-based controllers rely on soil moisture sensors to monitor the actual moisture levels, triggering irrigation only when necessary. Both types aim to enhance water efficiency and reduce waste, catering to residential and commercial landscaping needs.</p></p>
