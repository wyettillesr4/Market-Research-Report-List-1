<p><p>The industrial pumps market comprises various types, each serving specific applications. Centrifugal pumps utilize rotational energy to move fluids, ideal for high flow rates. Reciprocating pumps generate pressure through a back-and-forth motion, suitable for precise and high-viscosity fluid transfer. Rotary pumps harness the movement of gears or vanes, offering a consistent flow, often used in lubrication systems. Diaphragm pumps use a flexible diaphragm to create suction and discharge, effective for handling hazardous or corrosive liquids in various industries.</p></p>
