<p><p>Triple eccentric butterfly valves are designed to provide superior sealing and reduced wear through their unique geometry. The wafer type features a compact design, easily sandwiched between pipe flanges, making it suitable for space-constrained applications. In contrast, the lug type has threaded holes that allow the valve to be bolted directly to flanges, providing added flexibility for installation and maintenance. Both types effectively handle a range of fluids, making them popular in various industries for reliable performance.</p></p>
