<p><p>The chemical splash goggles market is divided into two main types: non-vented and indirect vented goggles. Non-vented chemical splash goggles are designed to provide a complete seal against liquid splashes, making them ideal for environments with hazardous chemicals where fogging is minimal. In contrast, indirect vented goggles feature vents that allow airflow to reduce fogging while still protecting against splashes. These types cater to different needs, balancing ventilation and protection based on the specific risks associated with chemical exposure.</p></p>
