<p><p>The Washing and Sterilizing Machine market features two primary types: Linear Tunnel Type and Rotary Type. Linear Tunnel Type machines operate through continuous conveyor systems, allowing for high-capacity washing and sterilization of various items in a streamlined process. In contrast, Rotary Type machines utilize a rotating drum mechanism, enabling effective cleaning and sterilization in smaller batches, which is ideal for more delicate or diverse items. Both types serve crucial roles in industries such as healthcare, food processing, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
