<p><p>Label-free detection systems are advanced technologies used to study biological interactions without the need for fluorescent or radioactive labels. The market is primarily divided into two types: biochemical assays and cell-based assays. Biochemical assays focus on analyzing interactions between biomolecules, such as proteins or nucleic acids, enabling researchers to understand molecular functions. Cell-based assays, on the other hand, assess cellular responses to various stimuli, providing insights into drug efficacy, toxicity, and cellular behavior in real-time without altering the biological sample.</p></p>
